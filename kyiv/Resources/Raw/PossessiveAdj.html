Possessive adjectives and subject pronouns

Subject pronouns Possessive adjectives
I have a brother Jack is my brother
You are Ukrainian Give me your book
He have a brother Jack is his brother
She is a student I know her sister
It is a bicycle It has its advantages
We have a house That's our house
They have a house That's their house

Be careful:

  1. it/its for things and they/their for people and things
  2. Its = possessive adjective. It’s = it is (subject + verb be)

    3. Look at that dog. It’s beautiful. I like its hair.
